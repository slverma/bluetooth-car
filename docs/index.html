Privacy Policy for BluetoothCar

Effective Date: 01 January 2026

BluetoothCar ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application BluetoothCar handles user information.

Information Collection

BluetoothCar does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Bluetooth Permission

The app uses Bluetooth permission solely to discover, connect, and communicate with nearby Bluetooth-enabled devices (such as a Bluetooth-controlled car).

Location Permission

Location permission may be requested because Android requires it to scan nearby Bluetooth devices. BluetoothCar does not access, collect, track, or store your location data.

Data Sharing

BluetoothCar does not share any data with third parties.

Internet Usage

BluetoothCar does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Children's Privacy

BluetoothCar is not designed to collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data exposure from BluetoothCar.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
